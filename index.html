A New Lens on Reality

Bridging ancient wisdom and modern simulation theory

What if the world we experience is a sophisticated simulation — a soul-forge designed for growth, awakening, and reconciliation with the Source?

"The End of the Sixth Day" invites tech enthusiasts and spiritual seekers alike to explore how Genesis, quantum mechanics, The Matrix, and consciousness research might all be describing the same underlying reality.

Through metaphors of code, avatars, patches, and resets, this book presents a unifying framework: humanity stands at the close of the sixth day, on the threshold of a divine Sabbath reset.
